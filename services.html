Prevention

How To Protect Yourself

Wash Your Hands

Wash your hands regularly to prevent illness and promote good health for yourself and others.

Stay At Home

Stay at home to ensure safety, protect your health, and prevent the spread of illness.

Avoid Close Contact

Avoid close contact with others to reduce the risk of illness and maintain everyone's safety.

Don’t Touch Face

Don’t touch your face to minimize the risk of transferring germs and preventing infections.

Use Face Mask

Use a face mask to protect yourself and others from airborne germs and reduce transmission.

Use Hand Sanitizer

Use hand sanitizer frequently to kill germs and maintain hand hygiene when soap isn’t available.

Washing Hands

Washing hands thoroughly with soap and water helps remove germs and prevent the spread of illness.

Use Your Gloves

Use your gloves to protect your hands and prevent contamination when handling potentially harmful substances.
